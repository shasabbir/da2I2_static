Building a Technology Stack

Implementing a System typically requires a range of different technologies, including: Machine learning and AI technology, Data collection and processing, Database management, Data visualization technology and Security. A comprehensive and well-integrated technology stack is essential to ensure that the system can collect, process, and present data accurately and reliably.

Television Rating Point (TRP) System

A jointly developed product by Bangladesh Satellite Company Limited (BSCL) and American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB).

    AIUB Research & Development:
  • An industry standard Audience End Device development & Production
  • An industry standard People’s Meter Development & Production
  • A central database with the capability of receiving data from TRP box and OTT platform
  • Graphical user interface (GUI) development

The TRP system relies on data collection and analysis to measure TV viewership. With the rise of advanced data analytics and machine learning, we can expect a more accurate and comprehensive measurement of viewership in the future.

In conclusion, the TRP system will need to evolve to keep up with the changing media landscape and advancements in technology. By embracing new technologies and methods, the TRP system can continue to provide accurate and comprehensive data on TV viewership in the future.
